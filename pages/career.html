Our Employment Philosophy

Our focus is on positively impacting the life of each child, through our commitment to see them reach their greatest potential. We also focus on providing a fun and supportive community for our employee's. Hear more about our Kudos program and how it helps bring a nurturing environment to the workplace.

Integrity

To conduct our business with integrity and efficiency while providing a pleasant and rewarding experience for all employees and clients.

Respect

To respect the individual rights of each employee while treating them with courtesy, dignity, and consideration.

Safe

To provide safe, healthy, and harmonious working conditions.

Highest Quality

To seek employees of the highest quality and select employees on the basis of their skills and abilities, which best match the job position.

Community

To continue to make Partners In Excellence a desirable place to work and a source of pride to our community.

Training

We offer competitive wages and excellent internal and external training opportunities.
